The field of artificial intelligence (AI) technology encompasses a broad spectrum of tools and methodologies. Among these, computer vision technology stands out as a prominent area of focus. Computer vision is intricately linked with other technologies such as machine learning (ML), deep learning (DL), artificial neural networks, and more. Its applications span across various domains, with healthcare emerging as a particularly significant sector in recent years. Within healthcare, computer vision algorithms leveraging the aforementioned technologies are employed to extract meaningful insights from medical imagery. This chapter provides an overview of computer vision, its diverse applications, and its specific utilization within the healthcare domain. Additionally, it explores an illustrative example of tumor detection through computer vision within a MATLAB environment.
